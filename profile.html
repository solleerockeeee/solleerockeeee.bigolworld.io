Making It All Personal

This Is How Your Profile Would Look On The bigolworld Platform


Your profile on bigolworld would include standard personalization features
as well as rather niche statistics, first off is there would be no "follower" system
as that promotes rather toxic influencer behavior. Instead going for a system
of a global like to dislike ratio. Causing users who are more universally liked
being more frequently seen on reccomended pages as that ratio paired with
global post quality are variables in users' discoverability scores.
