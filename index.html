Why bigolworld?


The reason for the creation of the bigolworld site
is that social justice and political opinions are being
brought into non-political matters, and causing individuals
to act on misinformation. Users need to see a bigger
world of opinions on a problem, and bigolworld's algorithm
aims to tackle that challenge.
