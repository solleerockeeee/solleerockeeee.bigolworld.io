Prompts & Problem Solving


The initial prompt used to ideate this concept was,
"Political spin affecting public knowledge of clean
energy." By researching the concept of political
spin, I had found out it was mostly influenced by
social media algorithms spreading incendiary political
viewpoints. And drawing users into echo-chambers wherein
a users own thoughts stop being subject to constructive
criticism.

The "Curiousity" algorithm is designed to expose users to
more opinions on a problem or topic by slowly easing them
in using the best posts relating to them

The "Quality" algorithm is designed to ensure the most credible
posts are rewarded, instead of relying purely on how engaging they
are.

^These Are The Two Main Algorithms That Power bigolworld^


All comments on the code are marked in green by hashtags
and explain how the components of the proposed algorithhm
help in achieving it's goals of favoring quality information,
and exposing the users to new opinions.
